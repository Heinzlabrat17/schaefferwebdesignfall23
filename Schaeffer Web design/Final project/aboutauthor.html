All About Morgan Wallen

5 Facts about Morgan Wallen

  1. Morgan was musically gifted from a young age he started singing at church at the young age of 3. In a 2021 interview with Nashville Lifestyles Morgan said he asked for a violin for Christmas when he was 5 years old. He also began playing the piano when he was 7.

  2. Morgan has two biological sisters (Ashlyne and Mikaela) as well as an adopted sister Lacey. His parents (Tommy and Lesli Wallen) adopted Lacey in 2021. Ashlyne provided vocals for the song “Outlook” on Morgans most recent album.

  3. Morgan became a dad on July 10, 2020, when his son Indigo was born in Nashville. Indigo’s mother is Wallen’s ex-girlfriend KT Smith.

  4. Morgan was a contestant on season 6 of the singing competition The Voice. He auditioned with a cover of Howie Day’s “Collide” and joined Team Usher. Maroon 5 frontman Adam Levine later stole Wallen for his team but he was eliminated in the playoff round.

  5. With all 36 songs off Wallen’s latest album Morgan now has 62 Billboard Hot 100 hits in his career. His first, “Up Down” featuring Florida Georgia was almost five years ago on April 7, 2018.
